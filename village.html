DISTRICT LOCATION

Sirmaur is the southernmost district of
Himachal Pradesh, northern India.
It is largely mountainous and rural, with 90% of
its population living in villages
. Some of its towns include Nahan (the capital),
Paonta Sahib and Suketi, the latter
known for the Shivalik Fossil Park.
